Privacy Policy for Taka Switch

Effective Date: June 20, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Taka Switch (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Taka Switch mobile application.

1. Information Collection

We collect the following information:

  • Google Play payment details (via Google Play APIs)

  • Withdrawal information (e.g., bKash, Nagad, or Binance numbers)

2. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Your information is used only for wallet and withdrawal operations.

4. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

5. Children’s Privacy

Taka Switch is not intended for users under 13 years of age.

6. Your Rights

You may contact us to request deletion of your account and related data.
